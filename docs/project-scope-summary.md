# Project Scope Summary

Tai lieu nay tom tat pham vi tu file PDF `NhomBaoTiep.pdf` de lam can cu to chuc cau truc du an.

## Muc tieu

- Xay dung nen tang thuong mai dien tu cho kinh mat
- Ho tro dat kinh theo don thuoc truc tuyen
- Ho tro thu kinh ao va goi y theo khuon mat
- Toi uu luong van hanh giua Sales, Lab va Logistics

## 5 nhom quy trinh nghiep vu

### 1. System Admin & Identity

- Quan ly tai khoan noi bo
- Phan quyen theo vai tro
- Dang ky, dang nhap, reset mat khau
- Quan ly ho so ca nhan va so dia chi
- Quan ly so tay don kinh, luu thong so mat va anh don thuoc

### 2. Catalog, Variant & Promotion

- CRUD gong kinh
- Quan ly bien the mau sac, size, stock, hinh anh
- Cho phep pre-order khi het hang
- CRUD trong kinh theo chiet suat, tinh nang, gioi han do
- Tao voucher, quan ly chinh sach bao hanh va doi tra

### 3. Shopping & Smart Suggestion

- Liet ke, loc, tim kiem san pham
- Goi y kinh theo khuon mat
- Cau hinh kinh thuoc step-by-step
- Gio hang, voucher, checkout, COD, VNPay Sandbox

### 4. Sales & Customer Service

- Sales xem don moi
- Tham dinh don kinh
- Xu ly don pre-order
- Quan ly khiếu nai, doi tra, bao hanh, hoan tien

### 5. Operations, Logistics & Dashboard

- Gia cong kinh theo luong thao tac
- QC
- Dong goi, tao ma van don, cap nhat giao hang
- Cap nhat kho cho hang pre-order
- Dashboard doanh thu, top san pham, ti le thanh cong / huy don

## Tac nhan chinh

- Customer
- Sales / Support
- Operations Staff
- Manager
- System Admin

## Cum bang du lieu chinh

- Roles, Users, Addresses
- Products, ProductVariants, Inventories, Lenses, Promotions
- Prescriptions, Carts, CartItems
- Orders, OrderItems, Payments, Shipments
- SupportTickets, ReturnWarranties
